## ICarePet
<b>ICarePet<b> is a next-generation pet shop management platform designed to improve the experience for both customers and their pets.
It combines modern technology and innovation to help pet shops deliver better service, smoother operations, and greater customer satisfaction.

Built with the future of pet care in mind, ICarePet focuses on comfort, autonomy, and smarter interactions between clients and pet shops.

### Key features

- Customer self-scheduling, allowing clients to book appointments at their convenience

- Service selection in advance, so customers can choose the type of care their pet needs before leaving home
